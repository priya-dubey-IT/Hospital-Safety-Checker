## Slide 2: Introduction & Objective
**Introduction:** 
The Hospital Safety Checker is a modern, full-stack hospital management system designed to streamline daily operations like patient tracking, doctor assignments, and clinical workflow management.

**Problem Statement:** 
Many medical facilities struggle with fragmented patient registration, inefficient queuing, and cumbersome manual reporting. This leads to operational bottlenecks and decreased efficiency for healthcare professionals.

**Objective:** 
To build a secure, lightweight, and powerful centralized platform that unifies patient registration, automates real-time doctor assignments, simplifies clinical data exports, and integrates AI-assistance for query resolution.

---

## Slide 3: Technologies Used & Research Methodology
**Technologies Used:**
- **Frontend:** React.js (Vite) for a modern, responsive User Interface.
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python) for high-performance API delivery.
- **Database:** SQLite for robust, local data persistence.
- **AI Integration:** Google Gemini for an intelligent medical and navigational chatbot.

**Research Methodology:**
- **Iterative Development:** Agile-based approach to simultaneously develop decoupled frontend and backend services.
- **System Architecture:** Decapsulated service layers separating API routing, business logic, data export, and AI integrations.
- **Quality Assurance:** Comprehensive automated testing (using PyTest) encompassing functional tests, system integration, and dependency vulnerability scanning to ensure medical-grade reliability.

---

## Slide 4: Future Scope
- **Cloud Migration & Scalability:** Transitioning from local SQLite to a distributed database (like PostgreSQL) and deploying on cloud platforms (AWS/Azure).
- **Advanced Predictive Analytics:** Incorporating Machine Learning models to forecast patient inflow, enabling optimal staffing and resource allocation.
- **EHR Integration:** Enabling seamless data syncing with global Electronic Health Record (EHR) standards.
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Expanding the system beyond doctors and admins to include tailored dashboards for nurses, pharmacists, and the billing department.
- **Mobile Application:** Developing native Android/iOS applications to give doctors on-the-go access and provide real-time status notifications to patients.

## Slide 5: Conclusion
- The **Hospital Safety Checker** successfully resolves traditional clinical bottlenecks by centralizing patient data and doctor workflows into a single intuitive platform.
- Features like the unified registration system and the automated waiting list significantly reduce manual overhead, ensuring patients are queued and treated efficiently.
